Comprehensive Care Across Multiple Specialties

Top Clinic brings together a diverse team of medical professionals covering a wide range of specialties to meet all your healthcare needs under one roof. Our key areas of expertise include:
  • General Medicine - Primary care and preventive health services.
  • Pediatrics - Compassionate care for infants, children, and adolescents.
  • Cardiology - Diagnosis and treatment of heart and circulatory conditions.
  • Dermatology - Expert solutions for skin, hair, and nail health.
  • Orthopedics - Care for bones, joints, and musculoskeletal issues.
  • Gynecology & Obstetrics - Women's health, pregnancy, and reproductive care.
  • Neurology - Specialized evaluation and management of brain and nerve disorders.
  • Psychiatry & Mental Health - Confidential and supportive mental wellness services.
At Top Clinic, we focus on integrated care, where specialists collaborate to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients.
